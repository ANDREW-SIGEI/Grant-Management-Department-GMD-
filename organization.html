Organizational Structure

Understanding the hierarchy and organization of KEMRI's Grant Management Department.

Department Hierarchy

Organizational Hierarchy

The Board of Directors, KEMRI

The highest governing body that oversees all KEMRI operations, including the Grant Management Department. They set strategic direction and approve major policies.

Director General & CEO

Reports to the Board of Directors and has overall responsibility for KEMRI's operations. The Director General oversees all departments, including the Scientific Programmes, Partnerships and Grants Management.

Director, Scientific Programmes, Partnerships and Grants Management

Leads the entire grants and partnerships functions at KEMRI, including the Grant Management Department. This position is responsible for ensuring effective management of research grants, scientific programmes, and strategic partnerships.

Core Units

Pre-Award, Contracts & Compliance Unit

Responsible for all activities leading up to grant award, including proposal development, submission, contract negotiation, and compliance oversight.

  • Pre-Award: Identifying funding opportunities and supporting proposal development
  • Compliance: Ensuring adherence to funder and institutional requirements
  • Monitoring & Evaluation: Tracking project implementation and outcomes

Deputy Director, Grants Management

Oversees the daily operations of grant management after awards are received, including financial management and administrative functions.

  • Human Resource: Managing staffing for grant-funded projects
  • Procurement: Handling purchasing of equipment and supplies for projects
  • Grants Finance (Post-Award): Managing financial aspects of active grants
  • Compliance: Ensuring ongoing adherence to grant terms

Partnerships & Resource Mobilisation

Focuses on establishing strategic partnerships and securing resources to support KEMRI's research agenda.

  • Partnerships: Developing and maintaining relationships with funding agencies and research collaborators
  • Resource Mobilization: Identifying and securing financial and other resources

Reporting Structure

The Grant Management Department follows a clear reporting structure to ensure effective communication and accountability:

1

Unit Staff

Staff members in each unit report to their respective Unit Heads.

2

Unit Heads

Unit Heads report to the Director of Scientific Programmes, Partnerships and Grants Management.

3

Director, Scientific Programmes, Partnerships and Grants Management

Reports to the Director General & CEO of KEMRI.

4

Director General & CEO

Reports to the Board of Directors, KEMRI.
