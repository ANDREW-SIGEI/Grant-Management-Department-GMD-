Grants Management Department

The Grants Management Department (GMD) at KEMRI is dedicated to supporting researchers in securing and managing grants for scientific research. Under the leadership of the Director of Scientific Programmes, Partnerships and Grants Management, our team of experienced professionals provides guidance throughout the entire grant lifecycle.

Our department is structured to offer comprehensive support across three main units:

  • Pre-Award, Contracts & Compliance Unit: Supporting proposal development, submission, and compliance reviews
  • Grants Management: Overseeing financial administration, procurement, and human resources for grants
  • Partnerships & Resource Mobilisation: Facilitating collaborations and identifying funding opportunities
