About Us

Learn about the Grant Management Department and our mission to support research excellence.

Our Mission

To facilitate and enhance the capacity of KEMRI researchers to secure and effectively manage research grants, ensuring compliance with funder requirements and institutional policies.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in research grant management, supporting KEMRI's position as a leading health research institution in Africa and globally.

Organizational Structure

The Grant Management Department operates under the oversight of the Director of Scientific Programmes, Partnerships and Grants Management, who reports to the Director General & CEO, and ultimately to the Board of Directors.

Key Divisions & Functions

Pre-Award, Contracts & Compliance Unit

Pre-Award

Identifying funding opportunities, supporting proposal development, reviewing budgets, and coordinating grant submissions.

Compliance

Ensuring adherence to funder regulations, institutional policies, and regulatory requirements throughout the grant lifecycle.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Tracking project implementation against milestones and deliverables, conducting progress reviews, and evaluating research outcomes.

Deputy Director Grants Management

Human Resource

Managing staffing requirements for research projects, facilitating recruitment, and coordinating capacity building activities.

Procurement

Handling acquisition of goods and services for grant-funded projects in accordance with funder and institutional requirements.

Grants Finance: Post-Award

Managing financial aspects of awarded grants, including budget monitoring, expenditure tracking, and financial reporting.

Compliance

Conducting regular audits and reviews to ensure adherence to financial regulations and grant agreement terms.

Partnerships & Resource Mobilisation

Partnerships

Establishing and maintaining collaborations with local and international research institutions, funding organizations, and other stakeholders.

Resource Mobilization

Developing strategies to attract and secure funding for KEMRI's research priorities and institutional development.

Our History

The Grant Management Department was established in 2010 as part of KEMRI's strategic plan to enhance research capacity and funding. Since its inception, the department has been instrumental in increasing the institute's success rate in securing competitive research grants and improving grant management practices.

Over the years, the department has grown from a small unit with three staff members to a comprehensive department that provides end-to-end support for all grant-related activities across KEMRI's research centers.

Today, the department plays a critical role in KEMRI's research ecosystem, contributing significantly to the institute's reputation as a center of excellence in health research in Africa.
